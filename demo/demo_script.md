# ProofPay AI - Demo Script

## Target Time

Under 2 minutes.

## Opening

"Many student buyers pay vendors through social media without knowing if the seller is trustworthy. ProofPay AI gives the buyer a trust signal before payment, then uses Kora to power the payment flow."

## Step 1 - Landing Page

"This is ProofPay AI. A seller can create a trusted payment request and send it to a buyer instead of asking for a blind transfer."

## Step 2 - Create Payment Request

"The seller enters the item details, amount, delivery method, and buyer information. The backend generates a payment request and calculates a trust score."

## Step 3 - Buyer Trust Page

"The buyer opens the link and sees the important details immediately: who the vendor is, how much they are paying, and whether the request looks trustworthy."

## Step 4 - Trust Score

"This vendor has a 95 out of 100 Trusted score. The reasons are explainable: complete profile, strong transaction history, no disputes, matching account name, and a normal amount for this category."

## Step 5 - Pay With Kora

"The buyer can now pay securely through Kora. If the sandbox webhook fires, the status updates automatically. If the sandbox is unstable during the presentation, we use our demo simulation endpoint to trigger the same paid status."

## Step 6 - Payment Confirmation

"Once payment is confirmed, the buyer and seller can see the payment status update to Paid."

## Closing

"AI verifies trust. Kora powers payment. ProofPay AI creates accountability."
